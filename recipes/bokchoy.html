Blanched Bokchoy

Description

Honestly pretty mid but it gets the job done.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 bag of bokchoy

Steps:

  1. Wash the bokchoy and cut in half.
  2. Boil water
  3. Place bokchoy in boiling water and let cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Drain water and serve. Extra yummers with a garlic sauce.
